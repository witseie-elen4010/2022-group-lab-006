Wordle Game Rules


Wordle is an online game in which you have six chances to guess a five-letter word. Here are the rules of playing the game:

Game rules for a single player mode:

  • You have to guess the Wordle in six goes or less
  • A correct letter turns green when it's in the correct position
  • A correct letter in the wrong place turns yellow
  • An incorrect letter turns gray
  • The ends after the six chances have been exhausted
  • When all the letters of the word are turned green, the guessed word is correct
